Wetnoose's Biography

BACKSTORY:

Wetnoose was an ordinary human from the planet Venus named John Wetnoose. He was the most popular kid in school, and everyone liked him. He even managed to go to Harvard.
One day, however, John was hit by a meteor and it completely destroyed Harvard and the rest of Venus. As the well-liked mascot of Harvard, John was trapped in his costume, which merged with him and transformed him into the coolest Hedgehog ever!!
Now known only as the mercenary Wetnoose, Wetnoose the Hedgehog became Sonic The Hedgehog's archrival after he killed Eggman and took over the world. Then he killed Sonic and now he is the king of the multiverse!!
